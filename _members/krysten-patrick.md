Krysten is the Research Coordinator for the Manske Lab. She completed her BSc in Kinesiology and Biomedical Sciences, followed by graduate work focused on a rural perspective of health care. Krysten's previous research focused on using large healthcare data to map care pathways and inform clinical guidance and health service development. Her background includes roles as a Research Coordinator in addictions medicine and paediatrics, as well as Epidemiologist and Analyst positions supporting health human resource and strategic planning at the hospital and provincial policy levels. In her free time, she can be found as far away from her laptop as possible—snowboarding, climbing, or paddling.
