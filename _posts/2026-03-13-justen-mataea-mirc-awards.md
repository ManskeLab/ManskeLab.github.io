Congratulations to Justen and Mataea for winning podium presentations at the McCaig International Research Conference - Advancements in Osteoarthritis: From Discovery to Treatment - this past weekend in Kananaskis!

Mataea won 3rd place for her presentation "Understanding pain in knee osteoarthritis before and after knee replacement: an arterial spin labeling analysis".

Justen won 2nd place for his presentation "RASL reconstruction leads to healthy wrist kinematics: implications for prevention of SLAC wrist osteoarthritis".

Congratulations Mataea and Justen -- we are excited to see your work progress!
